About Me

A Statistics and Economics student using web design and analytics to tell clearer stories about data and business.

Short Bio

My name is Tim, and I am a B.Sc. student in Statistics and Economics at St. Lawrence University in Canton, NY. Before that, I studied at UWC Atlantic in the UK and later joined an exchange program in Shanghai at East China Normal University, focusing on business, language and culture.

Outside class, I have worked in roles across finance, market analysis, and operations - from L'Oréal's supply chain to international cultural partnerships and marketplace analytics. These experiences made me appreciate how much good design and clear communication matter, whether in a dashboard, a slide deck, or a website.

I enjoy building things that are structured and practical, but still a bit fun: case decks, side projects, and now this portfolio for the Web Design Class.
